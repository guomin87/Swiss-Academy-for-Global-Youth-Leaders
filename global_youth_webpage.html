About Us

The Swiss Academy for Global Youth Leaders is a non-profit association based in Switzerland. We empower youth, especially from China and Switzerland, through leadership development, intercultural understanding, and educational exchange. Our mission is to shape globally minded leaders through hands-on experiences in academia, innovation, and global citizenship.

Programs

  • Summer & Winter Camps: Bilingual leadership programs in Zurich, Lucerne, and Lausanne.
  • STEAM Workshops: Innovation, sustainability, and university exploration.
  • School Exchange: Collaboration with Swiss high schools and universities.

Partners

We work with Swiss educational institutions, companies, and government foundations. If you wish to support global youth education, please get in touch.
